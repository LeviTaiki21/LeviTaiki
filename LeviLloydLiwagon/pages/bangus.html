BANGUS

Ingredients

  • Bangus
  • Vinegar
  • Peppercorns
  • Garlic
  • Salt

Procedure

  1. Clean and debone.
  2. Make the marinade- Combine the ingredients and stir until the salt dissolves.
  3. Marinade the fish- Do not marinate the bangus for too long, as the acids in the marinade will break down the proteins and make the flesh mushy.
  4. Carefully remove the duck eggs from the boiling water with the tongs then set them aside to cool.
  5. Fry the marinated fish- Drain the fish well to prevent splatters. Use the right kind and enough oil for the best results.
