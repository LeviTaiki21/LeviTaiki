PALABOK

Ingredients

  • Rice Noodles
  • oil
  • Ground pork
  • Egg
  • All-purpose flour
  • Lemon
  • Flakes

Procedure

  1. Soak the rice noodles in water for about 15 minutes. Drain and set aside.
  2. When the oil is hot enough, put-in the ground pork and cook for about 5 to 7 minutes
  3. Add the flour gradually while stirring.
  4. Meanwhile, boil enough water in a pot.
  5. Remove the strainer from the pot and drain the liquid from the noodles.
  6. Place the noodles in the serving plate.
  7. Pour the sauce on top of the noodles then arrange the toppings over the sauce.
