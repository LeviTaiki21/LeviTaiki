SALAD

Ingredients

  • Cucumber
  • Lettuce
  • Onion
  • Tomato
  • Lemon
  • Mayonaise

Procedure

  1. Wash all the vegetables throughly under running water wipe them dry.
  2. Peel and chop the other vegetables to bite sized piece.
