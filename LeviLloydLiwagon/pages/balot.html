BALUT

Ingredients

  • Duck Egg

Procedure

  1. Start by placing a small pot over medium-high heat.
  2. Let this heat up until the water starts to boil.
  3. Let this cook for at least 30 minutes.
  4. Carefully remove the duck eggs from the boiling water with the tongs then set them aside to cool.
  5. Let the duck eggs rest until they are cool enough to handle.
  6. Now take an egg and crack it open just a little bit while holding it upright.
  7. Serve immediately, while its still warm.
