LECHON BABOY

Ingredients

  • Pig
  • Lemon grass
  • Spring onions
  • Salt and pepper
  • Bay leaves
  • Garlic
  • Oil

Procedure

  1. Cleaning: Remove hair follicles from the pigâ€™s skin and the innards. Rinse the pig and clean the inside, wash and scrub the lumps of blood inside the stomach until thoroughly clean.
  2. Pat it dry and rub the pig with salt and pepper from inside and out. Brush the inside belly with enough or little soy sauce.
  3. Once done, stuff the belly with saba bananas (plantain), anise, green onion leaves, crushed garlic and laurel leaves. Stack it with a bundle of lemon grass in the stomach center.
  4. Prepare a mid size bamboo and skewer the pig and split roast over hot charcoal.
  5. Roast for a 3 to 4 hours until the meat is tender. Do not overcooked.
