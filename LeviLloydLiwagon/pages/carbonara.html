CARBONARA

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • All purpose cream
  • Garlic
  • ground meat
  • Onion
  • Any type of cheese

Procedure

  1. Cook the pasta by pouring water in a cooking pot. Let boil.
  2. One the onion softens, pour all-purpose cream into the pan.
  3. Put the cooked ground meat into the sauce. Stir.
  4. Add the cooked pasta. Toss until pasta gets totally coated with sauce.
  5. Add ant type of cheese of your choice. Toss. Add more cooked ground meat.
