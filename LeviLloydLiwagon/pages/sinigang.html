SINIGANG

Ingredients

  • Pork Belly
  • onion
  • Okra
  • Sinigang Mix
  • String beans
  • Water Spinach
  • Tomatoes

Procedure

  1. Boil the Pork belly in 2 quarts of water for 40 minutes.
  2. Pour the Sinigang Mix into a cooking pot. Let it boil and then add the onion, pork belly, and half the amount of the tomatoes.
  3. Add the long green pepper, string beans, remaining tomatoes, and okra. Cook for 3 minutes.
  4. Put the water spinach leaves. Cover and turn the heat off. Let the residual heat cook the leaves for 3 minutes before serving.
